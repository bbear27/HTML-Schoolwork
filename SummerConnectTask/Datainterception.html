Data Interception Attacks are when Unauthorised users acess private data.
This happenes in many different ways. 2 such examples are given Below:
Hijacking Software
Cyber Hijacking is when a hacker takes control of other computers, software and or a network.

Packet Sniffing
Sniffing attacks are data thefts caused by capturing network traffic with packet sniffers, which can
illegally access and read unencrypted data. When data packets travel through a computer network,
they are captured.
