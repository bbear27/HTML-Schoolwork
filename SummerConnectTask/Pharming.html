Pharming is a sort of social engineering hack in which attackers lead internet visitors seeking a certain website to a different,
bogus site. These "spoof" sites are designed to steal a victim's personal information, such as passwords,
social security numbers, account numbers, and so on, or to install pharming malware on their machine.
Pharmers frequently target financial-related websites, such as banks, online payment systems,
and e-commerce sites, with the goal of stealing personal information.
