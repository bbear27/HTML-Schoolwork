Phishing is a sort of cybercrime in which an attacker poses as a trustworthy entity and contacts victims by email, phone, or text message in attempt to
gain sensitive information or data, such as login passwords, credit card numbers, or other personally identifying information.
Phishing scammers will usually ask for the following information:
  • Year of birth
  • Social security number
  • Contact information
  • Information about your credit card
  • Address of your residence
  • Passwords
